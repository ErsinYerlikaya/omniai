Data & Knowledge Processing +

Data & Knowledge Processing

In most organizations, valuable knowledge is hidden in scattered files, emails, and legacy documents. We offer a comprehensive solution to unlock, organize, and operationalize this knowledge. Our AI-powered knowledge processing services help create internal hubs that improve access to strategic information and reduce dependency on individual expertise.

Using natural language processing (NLP), optical character recognition (OCR), and semantic search, we extract and structure information from unstructured sources — including PDF, Word, PowerPoint, and TXT files. These are indexed in vector databases like FAISS or Elasticsearch and made searchable through custom interfaces.

This enables fast onboarding of new employees, access to critical SOPs and project insights, and a reduction in duplicate efforts. Organizations benefit from increased resilience, improved documentation workflows, and a stronger foundation for data-driven decision-making.

Responsible & Compliant AI +

Responsible & Compliant AI

AI brings immense potential — but also serious risks. At Omni AI Solution GmbH, we ensure that your AI systems are not only powerful, but also ethical, explainable, and legally compliant. By working with us, you not only future-proof your systems against legal exposure but also increase trust among users, regulators, and partners. Responsible AI is no longer optional — it’s a competitive advantage and a safeguard for your reputation.

Custom Language Model Development +

Custom Language Model Development

Generic language models are powerful — but domain-specific models are transformative. We build custom large language models (LLMs) that are tailored to the linguistic patterns, terminologies, and document structures unique to your organization or sector.

We start by curating a high-quality dataset from your internal files, documentation, chat transcripts, or knowledge base. We then fine-tune models such as LLaMA, GPT-J, or open-source alternatives using advanced techniques like reinforcement learning with human feedback (RLHF), prompt tuning, or adapters. These models are then integrated into your infrastructure, either via API or on-prem deployment.

Applications range from document summarization and legal parsing to multilingual chatbots and semantic search engines. Businesses benefit from significant cost savings in customer service, compliance, and document analysis — while maintaining full control over the model's knowledge scope and security.
